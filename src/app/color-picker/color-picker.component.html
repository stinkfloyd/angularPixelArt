Welcome to the Angular Pixel Art Maker!

- You may change colors by clicking on the colored circle below.
- Custom colors may be saved by clicking "Add Color" on the left in the color chooser.
- You can click single pixels, or drag to paint with the selected color.
- Double-Clicking a pixel will erase the color.
- Clicking Quick-Fill changes the color of all squares.
- Changing the size of the grid will not clear your work.
